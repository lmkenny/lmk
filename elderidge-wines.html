Elderigde Wines

Graphic Design, Branding, Label Design

Elderidge Wines is a concept branding and labeling project for a winery based in the Barossa Valley, South Australia.

The inspiration of the brand is the rich cultural heritage of the Barossa Valley region. The objective was to combine tradition and innovation, sharing this history through contemporary visual language.

Motifs of the Arts and Crafts Movement and rough rustic textures are matched with contemporary typography and bold colours, creating a brand that speaks of the past in a contemporary tone. The range consists of the elegant Ashwood Cabernet Sauvignon, and the colourful Estate Shiraz and Riesling.
