The Golden Wattle / Restaurant Identity

Branding, Identity, Print Design

Adelaide bar/restaurant The Golden Wattle opened in 2018, taking over the space of a long established venue with a patchy reputation and limited audience. The aim of the branding was to create a new strong and unique identity that cleared the slate of the past, and connected with a new audience.

The Golden Wattle is styled on the classic Australian pub and aims to recall establishments of the past. The focus is a simple modern approach classic food, drinks and entertainment. The branding communicates this by takes cues from early 1900s Australian beer labeling and vintage signage of iconic Australian Pubs.

Since opening the venue has successfully established its identity with a new and diverse audience.
