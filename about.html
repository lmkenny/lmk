Liam Michael Kenny

I am a designer based in Sydney, Australia. I graduated in 2016 with a Bachelor of Media Design from Torrens University, Adelaide.

Displaying stylistic variety and a flair for colour, my work has been recognised with awards from the Australian Graphic Design Association, the Design Institute of Australia, and How Magazine.

I work across a varitey of mediums, specialising in digital illustration and animation, branding and packaging, and front end development.
