Liam Michael Kenny

I am a graphic designer based in Sydney, Australia. Completing a Bachelor of Media Design from Torrens University, Adelaide in 2016, I majored in Graphic Design and Motion Graphics.

Working across a variety of different media, I specialise in branding, packaging, illustration, animation, digital design and front end development. I enjoy working on complex technical and conceptual projects.

My work displays stylistic variety and a flair for colour, and has been recognised with multiple awards from the Australian Graphic Design Association, the Design Institute of Australia.
