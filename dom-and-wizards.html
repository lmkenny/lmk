Dom & The Wizards

7" Single Cover Artwork

Cover artwork for the debut 7" single released by Australian band Dom & The Wizards.

The artwork pays tribute to the ill fated atmospheric re-entry of Russian Cosmonaut Vladimir Komarov, referenced in lyrics on the singles’ A side.

Drawing on Russian Constructivist graphics and Space Race imagery, the artwork depicts the spacecraft “the Soyuz” hurtling towards the Earths atmosphere.
